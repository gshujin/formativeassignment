About us

We are a fashion activism movement working towards a global fashion industry that sustains and preserves the environment and values the environment over profit.


We are world citizens. We are a global movement of people who make the fashion industry work. We are the people who wear clothes. And we care.









Why is sustainability important?


The global fashion industry contribute massively and actively to global warming as they emit a hefty amount of greenhouse gases per year. One of the reasons is that the vast majority of our beloved clothes are petroleum-based and made from fossil fuels, including polyester, acrylic & nylon (check your clothing labels, you may be surprised). These materials require significantly more energy in the production phase than natural or recycled fibers.

We need to be more considerate of humanity and the environment. Fashion does not have to kill the environment. Our goal is to have a fashion industry which works without leaving a negative footprint.



TRANSPARENCY


Are brands publishing lists of their suppliers and how detailed is this information? Transparency is vital for holding major brands accountable for their human rights and environmental impacts across their supply chains.

Are brands sharing information such as:

  • Address of facility
  • Approximate number of workers
  • % of migrant and contract workers
  • Gender and age breakdown of workers

    • Without transparency, achieving a sustainable, accountable, and fair fashion industry will be impossible.
